About This Quiz

This quiz is not meant to judge productivity, but to offer gig workers a reflective experience on the value of their labor — economic and emotional. The goal is to challenge the idea that income alone defines worth.

Who is this for?

Gig workers — including delivery drivers, freelance designers, Uber drivers, online remote taskers (Fiverr, Upwork), and other platform-based laborers who experience work in a fractured, unstable, and often invisible system.
